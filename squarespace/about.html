Our Story

We opened our doors in July 2006 with one simple idea — a family restaurant serving fresh steak, seafood and chicken, alongside the home-made country cooking specials that taste just like Sunday at grandma's.

From the very beginning, The Surf and Turf Lodge has been family owned and family run. We take real pride in the quality of our food and the way we treat every guest who walks through the door. Whether you're grabbing a quick supper or feeding the whole crew, you'll get a fresh, home-cooked meal and a friendly welcome every time.

Our dining room seats over 200 guests and we're happy to accommodate large parties, family gatherings and celebrations — just give us a call and we'll take care of the rest.

On Thursdays and Sundays we fire up the country cooking — meatloaf, fresh vegetables, and all the home-style fixings, plus our Thursday 2-for-$28 dinner and a proper Sunday country lunch. Prefer to take it home? Order online or roll through the drive-thru, and we'll have it hot and ready. We'd love to serve you and your family soon.
